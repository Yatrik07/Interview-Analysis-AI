How It Works

Our platform provides a comprehensive evaluation of your mock interviews. Just upload a video, and our deep learning algorithms will analyze your performance to generate a data-driven report. We'll show you what you're doing right and where you can improve.

Language & Speech Analysis

We analyze your pitch modulation, clarity, and word choice using advanced Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) and NLP models to see if you sound confident and clear.

Emotion & Tone Recognition

Our CNN and BiLSTM models analyze your vocal tone and transcript to see if your emotional expression matches your message, helping you build trust and rapport.

Body Language Feedback

We track eye movement, facial expressions, and blinking patterns using OpenCV and Isolation Forests to improve your non-verbal communication.

Built on Open Source

This project fits proudly within the open-source domain. We believe in transparency, collaboration, and building tools that benefit everyone. The entire codebase is available on GitHub, and it utilizes powerful open-source technologies like TensorFlow, Keras, and OpenCV.
